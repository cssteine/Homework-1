About Connor

I grew up in La Motte, a small (population: 220), farm town in eastern Iowa . I am a 22 year old student at the University of Iowa. I am currently a fifth-year senior double majoring in MIS and accounting and I plan on graduating May 2013. In Spring 2011 I interned with McGladrey & Pullen, LLP and I received a full-time offer as an audit associate. I plan to sit for CPA next summer and will be starting with McGladrey in October of 2013. Prior to McGladrey I drove Cambus for about one year and I must say that it is a lot easier than it looks.

My initial intention when starting my accounting degree was to receive my Masters in Accountancy. However, in the fall semester of 2011 I realized and decided that switching to a double major in MIS would allow me to broaden my horizons a little more and allow me to graduate earlier than I anticipated. I have one semester under my belt as an MIS major and I am happy to say that I do not regret my decision.

I recently got engaged over the summer to my current fianceé, Emily Gerhke. She currently is a personal trainer at CRWC. She is certified in multiple forms of yoga and she has her Russian Kettlebell Certification. We recently went to Denver, Colorado for vacation which is where I proposed. We also did a lot of boating over the summer on my father's boat, which is one my favorite things to do. We spend most of our boating time on the Mississippi River. I also enjoy hanging out with friends and playing sports. My absolute favorite sport to play is golf. I was able to play a few rounds while in Denver and I could not have imagined more beautiful golf courses.

I hope you enjoyed my website. If you have any questions please feel free to contact me. If you REALLY enjoyed my website you can be my friend on Facebook.
